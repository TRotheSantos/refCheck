Our Motivation

In light of the growing complexity in verifying academic citations, we were driven to explore innovative solutions leveraging Large Language Models. Our aim is to streamline the process for researchers and academics, enhancing the accuracy and efficiency of reference validation.

Background Story

The project is integrated into the KIT project curriculum, providing students in the field of Business Informatics with hands-on experience and teamwork opportunities. Within this framework, specific groups of students are tasked with completing the project within an academic semester.

About Us

Comprised of 7 students enrolled in the Business Informatics program, we are united by our passion for technology and innovation. With the winter semester of 2023/24 marking our collaborative endeavor in software development, we are eager to apply our skills and knowledge to address real-world challenges in academia
